About US

Mr. Rosario Kalavina

Executive Director

Rosario Kalavina is the Executive Director of Future for All in Development (FAID), a non-profit organization dedicated to community development and social welfare. He holds a Bachelor's degree in Education Humanities from the University of Malawi and diplomas in Community Development, Project Management, and Human Resources. With experience leading projects such as Mtengo Wanga, Back to Life (Water and Sanitation), and Phungu Wanga, Rosario is committed to empowering marginalized communities and promoting social justice.
